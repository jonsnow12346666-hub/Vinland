FAQs

How do I start chatting?

Create a profile, tap any user card on the home page, and start a private chat.

Are my messages saved?

Yes — every message is saved in Firestore and cannot be deleted, keeping chats transparent.

How does random call work?

Tap the random video/audio button — we pair you with a random online user via WebRTC.

Can I block users?

Yes — open any profile and tap the block icon. Blocked users cannot message you.

Privacy Policy

We store your profile, messages, and media in Firebase/Cloudinary. Uploads are validated. We do not sell your data. You may request account deletion at any time via support.

Analytics are anonymous. Third-parties: Google Firebase, Cloudinary media CDN.

About Vinland

Vinland is an aesthetic Gen-Z anime social hub built for real connections — private chats, group rooms, random video calls, and 24-hour stories with that pure anime vibe.
